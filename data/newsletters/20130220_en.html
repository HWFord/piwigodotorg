Hello Piwigo user, you are receiving this email because you have signed up to the Piwigo Newsletter. Piwigo is the Free Open Source photo gallery software for the web.

Piwigo 2.4.7

Piwigo version 2.4.7 is out. 10 languages have been updated with new documentation pages. A vulnerability has been fixed if your Piwigo is installed on Windows (the vast majority of Piwigo galleries is installed on Linux) and we have increased security on LocalFiles Editor plugin. Read details on the Piwigo 2.4.7 Release Notes.

Follow the automatic upgrade procedure and run the most recent version of Piwigo.

Happy 1-clic upgrade!

Website in Turkish and Danish

Piwigo Team is proud to announce the opening of da.piwigo.org and tr.piwigo.org, the Danish (Dansk) and Turkish (Türkçe) presentation websites for Piwigo. Piwigo.org is now available in 12 languages while Piwigo itself is available in 50 languages.



Special thanks to Kaare, pointman (translators in danish) and LazBoy, hakanselcuk, Nakre (translators in turkish).

Enjoy Piwigo, God fornøjelse med Piwigo, Piwigo'nun keyfine bakin

Piwigo 2.5 is coming soon

We are currently preparing Piwigo 2.5, the next major version. You are invited to test version 2.5.0RC1, and very soon 2.5.0RC2. You can download it and install it on your hosting (but not on your live website) or you can simply browse live Piwigo 2.5 beta. Details on Piwigo 2.5.0RC1 announcement.

Among changes, you will find:


Pagination on albums, just like on thumbnails. You can modify the number of albums per page on screen [Administration > Configuration > Options > Display]


In the Batch Manager, you can filter photos on dimensions (width, height and ratio).


Better default icons for default theme Elegant. That's just the beginning, other icons must be improved too.

Happy beta tests!
