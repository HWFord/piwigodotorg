Thank you to all the generous donors

First of all, the Piwigo team wishes you a very happy new year 2020.
The year 2019 ended with a very good momentum from the community, and this announces great things for 2020!
Remember, on December 11th, we launched a call for donations to replenish the Piwigo association’s bank account, which was overdrawn.
In 48 hours, we received more than €6,000 in donations, and in total, nearly €8,000 was collected!

At the current rate, this will allow us to meet our expenses for about 3 years.

Piwigo 2020 survey

To start the year off on the right foot, we have decided to invite you, users, contributors, friends, members of the Piwigo community, to have your say.
We need to get to know you better and understand why you are loyal to Piwigo, and how we can make it even better.

It will only take a few minutes to complete this survey, but it will really help us!
We hope that many of you will participate. And as we value transparency, we will communicate the results of the survey.
