Hello Piwigo user, here comes fresh news about the Piwigo project. New maintenance release 2.5.3, work in progress for Piwigo 2.6 and information about Piwigo Showcase.

Piwigo 2.5.3

We are pleased to announce the availability of version 2.5.3. This maintenance release fixes 9 bugs, updates 20 languages and now supports 61 languages with 3 languages added:
  • British English (as opposed to American English) thanks to Cadencia and Tadjio
  • Brezhoneg thanks to yves.kerleguer
  • Bahasa Indonesia thanks to papua
Full details on the Piwigo 2.5.3 Release Notes.

Update Piwigo from your administration home page or follow the automatic upgrade procedure.

500+ galleries in Piwigo Showcase

Launched 2 years ago during Summer 2011, Piwigo Showcase is now containing more than 500 live Piwigo galleries from all over the world!




Piwigo Showcase is a nice way to discover what can be done with Piwigo. You can also find Piwigo galleries with your favorite subjects, such as architecture, Black & White or aviation.

Piwigo 2.6 in progress

Piwigo 2.6 is under active development. The first beta version 2.6.0beta1 was released in June and 2.6.0beta2 is planned for the next few days. The current improvements include:
  • many new methods in API (user and group management)
  • improvement in mobile theme SmartPocket
  • more robust email system
  • permission inheritance
  • many technical improvements (useful for plugin/theme creators)
Piwigo 2.6 is expected before the end of year 2013. Don't hesitate to help us with beta-testing.
