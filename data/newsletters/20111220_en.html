Hello Piwigo user, you are receiving this email because you have signed up to the Piwigo Newsletter. Piwigo is the Free Open Source photo gallery software for the web.

Piwigo 2.3.2

Piwigo 2.3.2 is out. We have fixed several bugs in this version, see full details on the Piwigo 2.3.2 Release Notes.

Please welcome a new language: Cambodia (by Vanndy). This new language makes Piwigo available in 45 languages! Congratulations to all translation team members for their continued effort.

And also a few small features:
  • a list of all triggers is available on any Piwigo installation
  • in the Batch Manager, the filter on tags get an option for "All tags" or "Any tag"
  • new album are automatically placed on top of the list
  • new option on API method pwg.images.add to let Piwigo generate websize photo and thumbnail
Follow the automatic upgrade procedure and run the most recent version of Piwigo. Have a nice upgrade!

Piwigo on Google+

We have created a page for Piwigo on Google+. If you're a user of this new social network made in Google, it's time to circle and +1 our page! And by the way, if you like the way Google+ shows thumbnails in album, take a look at the new Gthumb+ plugin for Piwigo.



Of course, we keep our Facebook page and our Twitter account. You're more than welcome for discussion with Piwigo team and other fans/followers. We're waiting for your feedback.

Social media are great places for showing your support and tell your friends about Piwigo. Your Like, Follow or +1 can truly help us to get increased visibility.
