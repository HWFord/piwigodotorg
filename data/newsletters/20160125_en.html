Hello Piwigo user, here are fresh news about the Piwigo project.

Happy new year 2016 with Piwigo

2015 was mainly the year we moved to Github, which is probably the biggest technical change we embraced for years. It was, and still is, a technical challenge. We're now more and more efficient with this new tool and new features are coming. What's in the overall roadmap for 2016?
  • releases 2.8 & 2.9 and maybe 2.10. We would like to shorten the release cycle. Less features on each release, but more often.
  • introduction of a Piwigo "directory", separate from the Piwigo "showcase". It will be easier to enter the Piwigo directory, compared to showcase.
  • get your Piwigo highlighted when participating to the Piwigo demo. The idea is to have high quality and fresh content in the demo and give credits to Piwigo community for that.
Happy new year 2016 with Piwigo :-)


Piwigo 2.8.0RC1

The wait is over. Piwigo 2.8 is coming! After our move to Github, things are now more stable and last month was much more active on coding! To make Piwigo 2.8 come quicker, we have decided to skip the beta versions and directly start with release candidates (RC stands for Release Candidate). This version 2.8.0RC1 is for test only, so don't install it on your live website.
