Piwigo 2.4 - 60 new features for your photo gallery

Piwigo, the free and open-source software which enables you to create your own photo gallery on the web, comes out tomorrow, Friday June 22nd, 2012, with a new version 2.4 which includes many new features.

Version 2.4 of Piwigo, prepared by more than ten developers and over one hundred translators, brings ​​major changes, a new design for existing features and numerous improvements to the user interface.

The new "multiple sizes" feature allows Piwigo to display your photos in nine sizes, from XXS to XXL, ideal for mobile devices, desktop computers and HDTVs. The new default theme "Elegant" is designed to be simple and modern. The mobile theme, introduced in version 2.4, is automatically loaded for visitors on iPhones, Android and other smartphones. Piwigo 2.4 also brings watermarks, a highly anticipated feature by professional photographers.
