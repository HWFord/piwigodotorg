Beta-testers wanted for Piwigo 2.10

Do you remember when we released the version 2.9? we told the community "design matters". You'll see in the version 2.10 that it's even more true. We've been focusing our efforts on modernizing our user interfaces.

We need your help to test this beta version and give us some feedback. The more users that will test it the more stable the finale release will be Remember we're still on beta versions which are meant to be used for testing only. Do not use it on your production website.


New overall design for administration pages, with new header/menubar/tabs. Here you can see the new dashboard with colors!

What are the main changes you can find in Piwigo 2.10, compared to 2.9?
  • Modus becomes the new default theme, with 8 new modern skins
  • The administration gets an overall redesign, in light and dark modes.
  • Upload form redesigned
  • Album manager redesigned
  • Dashboard redesigned
